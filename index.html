Phi Kappa Phi

Phi Kappa Phi is an academic honararry society for students who have
demostrated superior capabilities in all fields. Phi Kappa Phi became national in
1900 and has continued to grow since then.

Local chapters distributed approximately $250,000 annually in awards and
scholarships as well as support a variety of campus programs that carry out the
Society's aim of recognizing the promoting academic excellence.
